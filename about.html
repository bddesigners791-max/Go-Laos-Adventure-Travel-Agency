About Go Laos Adventure

Born in Vientiane. Made for Myanmar travellers.

Our story

Our story

Go Laos Adventure was founded with one simple idea: make it easy, safe, and affordable for Myanmar travellers to explore the beauty of Laos. We're based in Vientiane and work with trusted local drivers, guides, and hotels across Vientiane, Vang Vieng, and Luang Prabang.

From helping you book your first train ticket to photographing you at Nam Xay Mountain at sunrise — our small team is with you at every step, speaking Burmese, answering every question, and making sure every day feels like a highlight.
